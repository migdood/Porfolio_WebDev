The Heroes

Superman is a powerful superhero with extraordinary abilities, including superhuman strength, flight, and heat vision, dedicated to protecting humanity from evil.

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is a skilled detective and vigilante who fights crime in Gotham City using his intelligence, martial arts expertise, and high-tech gadgets.

Aquaman, the king of Atlantis, possesses the ability to communicate with marine life and control the oceans, defending both land and sea from threats.

The Flash, also known as Barry Allen, is the fastest man alive, harnessing the power of super speed to fight crime and protect Central City.

Wonder Woman, or Diana Prince, is an Amazonian warrior with incredible strength and courage, using her lasso of truth and indestructible bracelets to champion justice.

Cyborg, also known as Victor Stone, is a half-man, half-machine hero, equipped with advanced cybernetic enhancements that make him a formidable defender of justice.
